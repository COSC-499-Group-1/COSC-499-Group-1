User Guide

This is a complete guide for Streamline Athletes

Browsing and Joining Channels

  • Click on "Browse Channels" either in the left hand sidebar or by clicking your profile icon in the top right
  • A list of public channels will be displayed along with the amount of members in it
  • Note: Public channels are sorted by popularity according to the amount of members
  • By clicking on a channel you will be shown the channel description as well as have the ability to join the channel

Channel Chatbox

  • The channel chat box consists of the chat log as well as a right hand sidebar to display online/offline users
  • The channel description can be accessed by clicking the channel name at the top of the chat box
  • An invite can be sent to another user by clicking the send invite button in the top right of the chat box and then entering in the username
  • Any users profile can be accessed by either clicking their name in the chat log or the right hand sidebar
  • To mention another user in the channel, type "@" in the message box and a list of users will pop up
    • If you mention someone they will be sent a notification that you have done so
    • If you hover over your own message you have the ability to edit, delete, and react with an emoji to it
  • Deleting a message will delete your message from the chat box but will not delete it from the database
  • If you hover over another users message you have the ability to react with an emoji
  • You can either react with an emoji by selecting one or by clicking on an already existing emoji

Creating Channels

  • A channel can be created by clicking "Create Channel" in the left hand sidebar
  • Channel Name, Channel Type, and Channel Description are all required fields
  • The created channel will show up in the left hand sidebar under the appropriate tab
  • If you create a channel you will be granted admin privileges for the channel and a crown will be displayed beside your username in the Chatbox
  • Note: Private Channels will not be displayed in the Channel Browser and only people you invite will be allowed to join

Admin Abilities

  • If you are the admin of a channel a crown will be displayed beside your username in the Chatbox
  • Admins have the ability to edit the channel description and .....

Friends

  • Friends can be accessed by clicking the "Friends" tab in the left hand sidebar
  • A list of current friends will be displayed
  • Add a friend by typing in a username into "Add Friend..."
  • To remove a friend simply click the "X" beside their name
  • Note: A friends chat box is a private direct message with the other friend and no other users can be invited to it

Un-subscribing from a Channel

  • To unsubscribe from a channel simply click the "X" beside the channel name in the left hand sidebar
  • You will be prompted to confirm or cancel un-subscribing
  • If no other users are left in a channel the channel will be deleted

Profiles

  • Your profile page can be accessed by clicking your username in the top right and clicking "Profile"
  • Here you will have the ability to edit any of your information
  • To change your profile picture, simply hover over the profile picture and click "Select File"
  • Any info you haven't submitted in the registration form will be displayed as "N/A"
  • All your information on your profile page will be publicly visible to any other user
  • Note: Other users profile can be accessed by clicking their username/profile icon in any channel

Settings

  • Access settings by clicking your username in the top right and clicking "Settings"
  • Here you can turn on "Dark Theme" and also hide/show explicit content
  • You also can update your email and change your password

Notifications

  • Notifications can be accessed by clicking the "Notification Bell" in the top right corner
  • Here you will see your notifications sorted by "Public Invites", "Private Invites", "Friend Invites" and "General Notifications"
  • Public, Private, and Friend invites are invites from other users to channels they have created
    • You can either accept or deny these invites
    • Accepting will re-direct you to the appropriate chat box and denying will not make you join the channel
  • General notifications are notifications of users mentioning you in channels or of users accepting/denying your invites
    • Clicking the message of the notification will result in you being re-directed to the channel and clicking the "X" will get rid of the notification
  • If you have unread messages in a channel a red circle with the number of notifications will be displayed beside the channel name

Logout

  • To logout simply click your username in the top right and click "Logout"
  • You will be redirected to the login screen
